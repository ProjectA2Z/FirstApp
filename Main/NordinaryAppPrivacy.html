Introduction

Welcome to Nordinary. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle user data in relation to the features and permissions required for the app to function effectively. Nordinary is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal data is handled in a safe and responsible manner.

Permissions Used and Their Purpose

1. Storage Access (android.permission.WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE & android.permission.READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE):

Purpose: We use storage permissions to download files from the internet and save them on your device. This is necessary for the app to provide tools and resources that you can access offline.

Data Handling: No data is transferred to our servers. All files downloaded remain on your device.

2. Text-to-Speech (TTS) & Speech-to-Text (STT) (android.permission.RECORD_AUDIO):

Purpose: These permissions allow the app to convert text to speech and vice versa. This feature is essential for users who need accessibility support or prefer voice interactions.

Data Handling: The audio data used for these features is processed locally on your device. No audio recordings or transcriptions are stored or transmitted.

3. Make Phone Calls (android.permission.CALL_PHONE):

Purpose: This permission allows users to initiate phone calls directly from the app. This feature is provided for convenience, such as contacting support or other necessary phone interactions.

Data Handling: No call data, phone numbers, or other related information is stored or transmitted by the app.

4. Internet Access (android.permission.INTERNET):

Purpose: Internet access is required for downloading resources, updates, and tools provided by the app.

Data Handling: The app does not collect or transmit any personal data over the internet. It solely accesses the internet to download the necessary resources for its functioning.

Data Collection and Usage

Nordinary does not collect, store, or share any personal data from users. All processing is done locally on your device. The app does not track your activities, and no information is shared with third parties.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
